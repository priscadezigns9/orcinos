Software for the Modern Pocket.

Orcinos is a powerful, pocket-sized suite of sovereign applications. Designed for clarity, with high-fidelity logic that makes sense at a glance — or a tap.

Privacy First

Data stored on-device.

Credentials and data are stored on-device, not on an Orcinos server. Connections are made directly between your phone and your nodes. As part of our commitment to sovereignty, Orcinos avoids any third-party tracking, analytics, or surveillance.

Nodes

Meticulously refined nodes — active at scale.

Orcinos makes complex software simple when you're away from your desk — whether you're building something new or shipping at scale.

Intelligence

Formatting that makes data human-readable.

Make raw node data much easier to read with custom formatting. Convert complex outputs, clean up telemetry, linkify protocols, and give booleans more human-friendly labels. All without modifying the source.

UX

Stay in flow.

Move through your division quickly with Node Flow. Swipe through active nodes and tap to jump right in — fast, frictionless, and built to speed up your workflow.

Security

Query carefully. Deploy safely.

Orcinos keeps production safe with built-in Deployment Safety. Set rules that ask for confirmation before edits or deletions. Block dangerous actions entirely. You always know where you are — before things go sideways.

Mobility

Works even when your connection doesn't.

Optimized for speedy asset access by storing some of your data on device. And with Offline Mode, Orcinos lets you access your critical division data without a working internet connection.

Collaboration

Share assets with an Expiring Link.

When it's touch-and-go, share rows with links that vanish after a peek — or on a timer. An upgrade from copy and paste.

Monitor

Notifications keep tabs on your division.

Global alerts monitor every table and node — so you can close the tabs kept in your browser. Real-time intelligence, delivered natively.
