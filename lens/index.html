What It Does

Six Modules. One System.

Every layer of traffic intelligence built into a single, deployable platform.

Live Vehicle Detection

Identifies cars, trucks, and motorcycles in real time from any camera feed using on-device AI — no cloud dependency.

Adaptive Signal Control

Green time extends or shortens automatically based on real-time lane density — no manual intervention required.

Violation Flagging

Any vehicle crossing the stop line on red is flagged instantly with a timestamp, frame capture, and alert.

Plate Recognition

OCR reads and logs license plates from violation frames automatically — building a searchable enforcement record.

Congestion Heatmap

A live visual pressure map of the intersection — updated every 5 seconds, showing which lanes need attention.

Exportable Reports

Full violation and density logs with timestamps, exportable on demand for enforcement action or compliance reporting.
