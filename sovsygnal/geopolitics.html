Israel & Lebanon Sign US-Brokered Framework Agreement — Hezbollah Condemns Deal

Israel and Lebanon agreed to a renewed ceasefire framework following US-brokered talks, with provisions to establish "pilot zones" in southern Lebanon designed to separate military forces. Hezbollah condemned the agreement, calling it a capitulation. Israeli tanks were still operational on southern Lebanese roads as of June 27, raising questions about implementation timelines and whether the deal can hold without direct Hezbollah participation.
